About Alpacas

Hi, my name is Kai, I am an Alpaca Enthusiast. Welcome to my site. In this website I will talk about Alpacas. Alpacas are native to the Andes Mountains of Peru, known for their soft fleece and friendly nature. They’ve been part of Peruvian culture for thousands of years and are a symbol of tradition and sustainability.
